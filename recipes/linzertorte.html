Linzertorte

My mum's incredibly delicious Linzertorte

I honestly don't know what to say here, except: Try and be amazed ;-)

Ingredients

For the dough

225g ground hazelnut

200g sugar

225g wheat flour

225g butter

2 tbsp Kirschwasser

1 tsp cinnamon

1 egg

1 tsp drinking chocolate powder

1 pinch backing powder

For the topping

1 1/2 glasses raspberry jam

2 eggs

Steps

Mix all ingredients for the dough and knead well until you have a smooth and even consistency.

Now wrap the dough in plastic wrap and put it in the refridgerator for a couple of hours.

Preheat your oven to 180°C.

Cut the cold dough in two halfes. Spread one half evenly inside a round springform pan and form a small edge around the sides.

Now cover the cake base with the raspberry jam.>

Roll out the remaining half of the dough and cut it into stripes to lay on top of the raspberry jam forming a grid.

Separate the egg yellow and whites and use the yellows to coat the dough grid.

Put the cake in the oven and bake it at 180°C for about 35 minutes. Then turn off the heat and let the cake sit in the oven for another 7 minutes before taking it out.

Let the cake cool off and you're done!

Enjoy! :-)
